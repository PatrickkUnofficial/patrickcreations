ALONE

A reflection on solitude, self-discovery, and what it means to be the last person on Earth
Written by Emmanuel Patrick Published on December 27, 2024 4 min read

If I was the last man on earth what is the first thing I would do? Firstly I would wonder for a while. I would wonder where everyone went, and why they didn't take me along. I would wonder where everywhere is so quiet. Eventually I would tell myself, "They'll be back?" then go on about my day. I would live as though nothing happened.

The first few days I wouldn't care much, at least that's what I would tell myself in murmurs, anger steaming inside. I would be mad that I wasn't included, I would hate myself, I would hate the world, but I must live on, musn't I?

I would comfort myself with the fact that I would have as much food as I want and I would never have to wait in lines ever again, and I would never be second place in a race, or be told what not to do. I could do whatever I wanted.

I would travel the world and all that it has to offer. But when all these things come to an end and my time can no longer be occupied, I would settle with the silence and cry. I would be sad. I would be scared and depressed.

We came into this world alone, we will leave this world alone, but for as long as we are in this world, no-one should be left alone.
— Alone

Once I have cried till no more tears, my eyes cannot carry any more bags and my lips cannot frown any further, I would accept where I am and what I have become. I would learn this new lifestyle and learn to live in it.

It would not be easy and I would surely lose some aspects of myself, but I will wake up the next day and ask myself, "So what am I doing today?"

Alone is the story of not a single person, but all of us in some way. It is about the solitude of every person and how even in a room full of people, one can feel so lonely.
