The Company

Patrick Creations is a story studio. A home for bold films, abstract thoughts, and emotionally charged narratives. We do our best. Every project, whether 30 seconds or 3 hours, is handcrafted. Purposeful. Raw. We're not here to fit in. We're here to show the wrld our truth and hope you'll agree.

The Founder

Patrick is an artist. A filmmaker. A storyteller obsessed with depth, silence, and soul.

He founded Patrick Creations to build stories from scratch. Work that is original and personal.

Inspired by the likes of Basquiat, Fincher, Chazelle, and truth itself, Patrick's work blends raw feeling with distinct visual identity. He's not just making art — he's building a language.
