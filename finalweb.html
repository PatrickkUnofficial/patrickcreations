WE ARE

Patrick Creations exists beyond the frame. We believe stories should challenge, question, and transcend the moment. This isn't just a company. It's a movement.

Founder's Welcome

Welcome to Patrick Creations. We believe in story. We beleive this is where vision, heart, rebellion, and grace all sit in the same room. We are honourned to here and to be given the opportunity to share our story. We don't chase trends. We make culture and what just feels right for us. Thank you for stepping inside our world and we hope you'll never want to leave.

— Patrick

The Company

Patrick Creations is a story studio. A home for bold films, abstract thoughts, and emotionally charged narratives. We do our best. Every project, whether 30 seconds or 3 hours, is handcrafted. Purposeful. Raw. We're not here to fit in. We're here to show the wrld our truth and hope you'll agree.

The Founder

Patrick is an artist. A filmmaker. A storyteller obsessed with depth, silence, and soul.

He founded Patrick Creations to build stories from scratch. Work that is original and personal.

Inspired by the likes of Basquiat, Fincher, Chazelle, and truth itself, Patrick's work blends raw feeling with distinct visual identity. He's not just making art — he's building a language.

The Brilliant Boy

2025 • 13m 27s Drama Short Film

In a world that fails to see beyond his autism, a brilliant young boy's untimely death robs the world of his extraordinary genius, leaving his potential forever unrealized. Nathaniel sees patterns and solutions invisible to others, but his unique way of thinking is misunderstood by those around him.

ALONE

2025 • 4m 40s Drama Short Film

Alone is the story of not a single person, but all of us in some way. It is about the solitude of every person and how even in a room full of people, one can feel so lonely. Beyond that, it also touches on themes of mental wellness and the need to care about not only our own mental health, but also that of the people around us.
